Overview

Accidentally in Love is a Drama/Comedy about Chen Qing Qing and Situ Feng's relationship at their college.

Plot

Chen Qing Qing is from a rich household, and ran away to college to avoid an arranged marriage. She goes to the college to learn more about her father and meets Situ Feng, who is an idol and singer. She helps him with school, manageing fans, etc and they and their freinds go on adventures throughout their time at school.
